**Overview**

This interactive dashboard provides insights into Mobiles’s sales performance across India. It tracks total sales, transactions, quantities sold, payment methods, customer ratings, and performance by mobile model, city, and day.
The dashboard allows users to filter data by month, making it easier to analyze trends over time.

**Key Metrics**

**Total Sales**: ₹68.12M
**Total Quantity Sold**: 2,000 units
**Total Transactions**: 325
**Average Sale per Transaction**: ₹41.73K

**Dashboard Components**

**Monthly Filter**:
Sidebar allows filtering sales data by month (January–December).
Current selection: May.

**Total Sales by City**:
A map visual showing the distribution of sales across major cities in India.
City size correlates with total sales volume.

**Total Quantity Sold by Month and Day**:
Line chart representing daily sales quantities throughout the selected month.

**Transactions by Payment Method**:
Pie chart showing distribution of transactions via Debit Card, Cash, UPI, and Credit Card.

**Customer Ratings**:
Bar chart counting customer ratings from 1 to 5.
Provides insight into customer satisfaction.

**Total Sales by Mobile Model**:
Horizontal bar chart comparing sales of Vivo S1, Galaxy A51, and iPhone SE.

**Total Sales by Day Name**:
Column chart showing sales trends for each day of the week.
Highlights peak sales days.

**Total Sales by Brand**:
Table showing sales figures for top brands: Apple, OnePlus, Samsung, Vivo, Xiaomi.

**Insights**

Highest Selling Brand: Samsung (₹15.3M)
Popular Payment Method: Debit Card (31.08% of transactions)
Peak Sales Day: Tuesday (₹11.6M)
Top Cities: Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi

**Technologies Used**

Microsoft Power BI 
Map and Chart Visualizations
Interactive filters for month selection

**Usage**

-Select a month from the sidebar to update all charts.
-Hover over charts for detailed data points.
-Analyze sales patterns by city, model, day, or payment method.
